Yoga

One of my favorite past time activities is yoga. I think I found a passion for yoga because it is a great way to relax and unwind while still getting a workout. When I get on my mat I am able to block any worries or stress that I have from coming into my mind during my practice. I stress very easily and I get a lot of anxiety when I have too much going on in my life and yoga has taught me how to balance things out so I can be happier and more relaxed.
